About Us

Metamorphosis Engitech India Private Limited, established in 2018, is an IATF 16949:2016 & ISO 9001:2015 certified leader in precision welded tubes. We manufacture and export a wide range of products—ERW, CDW/DOM, and more—serving sectors from automotive and general engineering to power, construction, and mining. With globally patented cold-pilgering technology and state-of-the-art facilities near Pune, we deliver high quality, innovation, and customer-centric solutions.
